Bitcoin Cash Node (BCH)

Bitcoin Cash Node (BCH) is the original Bitcoin Cash client that caused the split away from the Bitcoin blockchain in 2017.

BCH focuses it’s entire chain on an adjustable blocksize cap. This allows all node operators to adjust their block size limit quickly and easily, with the end goal of removing the block size limit entirely.

With a new direction and driven team, Bitcoin Cash strives to pave its own success and be a key player in the Crypto Industry.
